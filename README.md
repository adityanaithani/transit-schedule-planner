# Headway

n. the time interval between two vehicles traveling in the same direction on the same route

## the problem space: nonexistent trip scheduling

As a young person, I have quite an inconsitent daily schedule. An 8AM class on Monday, 12PM shift Wednesday and 5pm hangout on Friday means I almost never take the same bus or train every day.

However, even though i know a week prior where I'm going and when, I have to wait until the moment I leave the house to open [Transit](https://transitapp.com/) (one of my favourite products) and set up my trip.

What if i could schedule all my trips in advance? I wanted to see if I could build a concept of this feature.
